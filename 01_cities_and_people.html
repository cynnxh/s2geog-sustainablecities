Rural vs Urban

Tap each card to flip it and reveal how rural areas compare to cities.

👥Population Size
Rural: smaller (usually)
Urban: larger (usually)
📍Population Density
Rural: lower
Urban: higher
💼Main Economic Activity
Rural: primary (mining, farming)
Urban: secondary (manufacturing) & tertiary (services)
🏪Services
Rural: simple (provision shops, clinics)
Urban: complex (hypermarkets, large hospitals)
🌳Environment
Rural: natural environment retained, usually less pollution
Urban: built-up, usually more pollution
🚦Infrastructure
Rural: simple infrastructure
Urban: extensive infrastructure — public transport networks, comprehensive sewage systems
